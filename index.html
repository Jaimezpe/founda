About us

Founda is a website designed to provide programs and systems that help people improve their computers without spending any money, as well as tools for customization. Most of the programs available on Founda are developed by us, except for modifications, which are custom versions of existing software, and open-source programs that serve as complementary tools. Our goal is to offer useful and accessible solutions for users who want to enhance their devices or personalize their experience without extra costs.
